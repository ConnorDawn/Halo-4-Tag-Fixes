These are instructions you can follow yourself to fix your lowered weapon animations when moving or turning.


<----- RIFLES ----->

overlay group 348 "combat_rifle_aim_locomote_up"
add new blend screens "combat:rifle:aim_still_down"

overlay group 350 "combat_rifle_aim_turn_up"
add new blend screens "combat:rifle:aim_still_down"
